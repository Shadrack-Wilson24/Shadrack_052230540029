FACULTY OF APPLIED SCIENCES & TECHNOLOGY

The Faculty of Applied Sciences and Technology was Carved from the then Faculty of Applied Sciences in October, 2016. The reason for renaming of the Faculty was to help introduce new technology-related programmes and also to properly manage the existing ones in the institution.
